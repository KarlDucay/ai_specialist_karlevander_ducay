# Atlas Labs — Expense & Travel Policy (Internal)

## General principle
Spend company money as if it were your own. When in doubt, ask your manager before spending.

## Reimbursable expenses
The following are reimbursable with an itemized receipt:
- Business travel (flights, trains, ground transport).
- Hotels, up to **$250 per night** in standard metros; up to $350/night in New York, San Francisco, and London.
- Meals while traveling, up to **$75 per day**.
- Conference and training fees pre-approved by your manager.

## Non-reimbursable
- Alcohol (except at pre-approved team events).
- In-room hotel entertainment, minibar, and spa.
- Personal travel extensions or companion travel.

## Submitting expenses
Submit expenses through the finance portal within **30 days** of the expense date. Reimbursements are paid in the next payroll cycle after approval. Expenses submitted more than 60 days late require VP approval.

## Travel booking
Flights should be booked at least **14 days in advance** when possible. Economy class is standard for flights under 6 hours; premium economy is permitted for flights over 6 hours.

_Note: this policy does not cover corporate card issuance or per-diem rates for international assignments longer than 30 days._
