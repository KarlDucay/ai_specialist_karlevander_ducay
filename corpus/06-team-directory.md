# Atlas Labs — Employee Handbook (Internal)

_Last updated: March 2025. This handbook applies to all full-time Atlas Labs employees._

## Working hours
Atlas Labs operates on a core-hours model. All employees are expected to be reachable between **10:00 AM and 3:00 PM Pacific Time**, Monday through Friday. Outside core hours, employees set their own schedule. The standard work week is 40 hours.

## Remote and hybrid work
Atlas Labs is **remote-first**. Employees may work from anywhere within the United States. Employees based within 40 miles of the San Francisco office are asked to come in **one day per week**, on Wednesdays. Fully remote employees are not required to come in but may use the office whenever they wish.

## Paid time off
Full-time employees accrue **20 days of paid vacation per year**, accruing at 1.67 days per month. Unused vacation rolls over, capped at **30 days** total. In addition, employees receive **10 paid company holidays** per year (listed in the shared calendar) and **unlimited sick leave**, used in good faith.

## Equipment
New employees receive a company laptop (MacBook Pro, 16-inch) and a **$500 one-time home-office stipend** for desk, chair, or peripherals. Equipment requests beyond the stipend require manager approval.

## Performance reviews
Formal performance reviews happen **twice a year**, in June and December. Reviews are written by the employee's direct manager and discussed in a 1:1.

_Note: questions about compensation bands, equity, or parental leave are handled directly by People Ops and are not covered in this handbook._
