# Atlas Labs — Security & Data Handling Policy (Internal)

## Data classification
Atlas Labs classifies all data into three levels:
- **Public** — marketing material, published docs. No restrictions.
- **Internal** — handbooks, roadmaps, this policy. Shareable within Atlas Labs only.
- **Confidential** — customer data, model weights, financials. Access on a need-to-know basis.

## Customer data retention
Customer conversation data processed by Beacon is **retained for 30 days** by default, then permanently deleted. Enterprise customers may configure retention between **0 and 90 days**. Atlas Labs never uses customer conversation data to train its models.

## Access control
All production systems require **multi-factor authentication**. Access to Confidential data requires manager approval and is reviewed quarterly. Production database access is restricted to the platform engineering team.

## Incident response
Suspected security incidents must be reported to **security@atlaslabs.example** within **1 hour** of discovery. The on-call security lead acknowledges within 30 minutes. A post-incident review is completed within 5 business days.

## Acceptable use
Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled and screen-lock set to 5 minutes. Installing unapproved software that handles Confidential data is prohibited.

_Note: this policy does not cover physical office security or visitor access procedures._
