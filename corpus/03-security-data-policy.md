# Atlas Labs — Team Directory & Ownership (Internal)

_Use this to find who owns what. Names are role-based contacts, not individuals._

## Teams and what they own
- **Platform Engineering** — owns Beacon's backend, retrieval pipeline, and production infrastructure. Owns all production database access.
- **Applied AI** — owns prompt design, model evaluation, and answer-quality metrics for Beacon.
- **Product** — owns the roadmap, pricing tiers, and customer-facing features.
- **People Ops** — owns hiring, onboarding, compensation, and benefits.
- **Customer Success** — owns onboarding of Enterprise customers and the dedicated success-manager relationship.

## Key contacts
- Production incidents → **on-call rotation**, paged via the incident tool.
- Answer-quality regressions in Beacon → **Applied AI** team channel `#beacon-quality`.
- Pricing or contract questions → **Product** via `#pricing`.
- IT and account provisioning → it-help@atlaslabs.example.
- Security incidents → security@atlaslabs.example.

## Decision ownership
- Changes to Beacon's retrieval threshold are decided by **Applied AI**, reviewed with Platform Engineering.
- Changes to pricing tiers are decided by **Product** with Finance sign-off.
- Adding a new supported language is a joint decision between **Applied AI** and **Product**.

_Note: this directory does not list individual employee names, reporting lines, or org-chart hierarchy._
