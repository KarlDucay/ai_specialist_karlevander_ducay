# Beacon — Product Overview (Internal)

## What Beacon is
Beacon is Atlas Labs' flagship product: an AI-powered customer-support assistant that companies embed in their help centers. Beacon answers customer questions using the company's own documentation and escalates to a human agent when it can't.

## Key facts
- Beacon launched in **public beta in November 2024** and reached **general availability in February 2025**.
- Beacon supports **English, Spanish, French, and German** at launch. Japanese is on the roadmap for Q3 2025.
- Beacon retrieves answers only from a customer's approved knowledge sources and **cites the source document** for every answer it gives.
- When Beacon's confidence is below threshold, it does **not** guess — it routes the conversation to a human agent.

## Pricing tiers
Beacon has three tiers:
- **Starter** — $99/month, up to 1,000 resolved conversations.
- **Growth** — $499/month, up to 10,000 resolved conversations.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing, unlimited conversations, SSO, and a dedicated success manager.

A resolved conversation is one Beacon handles end-to-end without human escalation. Escalated conversations are **not** counted against the monthly limit.

## Integrations
Beacon integrates with Zendesk, Intercom, and Salesforce Service Cloud out of the box. A REST API is available on the Growth and Enterprise tiers.
